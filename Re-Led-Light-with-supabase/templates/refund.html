Refund Policy

Last updated: {{ moment().format('MMMM DD, YYYY') }}

1. Return Eligibility

Items are eligible for return within 30 days of purchase if they meet the following conditions:

  • Items are in original, unused condition
  • All original packaging and accessories are included
  • No signs of damage or wear
  • Valid proof of purchase is provided

2. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:

  • Custom or specially ordered products
  • Installed products (unless defective)
  • Items damaged by misuse or normal wear
  • Products modified or altered by the customer
  • Sale or clearance items (unless defective)

3. Return Process

  1. Contact Us: Email support@reledlight.com or call +91 12345 67890
  2. Return Authorization: We'll provide a Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number
  3. Package Items: Securely package items with RMA number clearly marked
  4. Ship Back: Send to our returns processing center (address provided with RMA)
  5. Inspection: We'll inspect returned items within 5-7 business days

4. Refund Timeline

  • Processing: 5-7 business days after we receive your return
  • Refund Method: Original payment method used for purchase
  • Credit Card: 3-5 business days to appear on statement
  • Bank Transfer: 5-10 business days

5. Shipping Costs

  • Defective Items: We cover return shipping costs
  • Customer Returns: Customer responsible for return shipping
  • Wrong Item Sent: We cover all shipping costs

6. Exchanges

We offer exchanges for:

  • Defective products
  • Wrong items shipped
  • Size or specification changes (subject to availability)

7. Warranty vs. Returns

Items with manufacturing defects after 30 days are covered under manufacturer warranty, not our return policy.

8. Refund Amounts

  • Full Refund: Product cost (minus original shipping if applicable)
  • Restocking Fee: 15% may apply to opened electronic items
  • Partial Refund: For items not in original condition

9. Installation Services

Installation service fees are refundable only if service was not provided or was unsatisfactory as determined by our quality review.

10. Bulk Orders

Special return terms may apply to bulk or commercial orders. Please refer to your purchase agreement or contact our commercial sales team.

11. Contact for Returns

For all return inquiries:

  • Email: returns@reledlight.com
  • Phone: +91 12345 67890
  • Hours: Monday - Saturday, 9:00 AM - 6:00 PM IST
Important Note
